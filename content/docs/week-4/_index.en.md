In week 4 I used Helm, the package manager for Kubernetes. Instead of applying individual YAML files manually, everything is bundled into a chart. I created a custom chart, installed it, upgraded it to a new version, and rolled it back.

Alongside Helm I worked through IAM using the EHR Healthcare case study: what are the capabilities of Azure IAM and what should a company like that actually deploy in practice?
